# College Board — Permission Request (draft)

**Goal:** ask College Board for written permission to display SAT Suite Question
Bank questions inside AceBoard. This is the one route that makes in-app use of
their questions legitimate. Submit via their official form:

- **Form:** Copyright & Trademark Permission Request — https://privacy.collegeboard.org/copyright-trademark/request-instructions
- **Processing time they cite:** ~4–6 weeks
- **Reality check:** approval for a third-party app that *redistributes*
  questions is a long shot (their policy says SAT test materials are not offered
  for commercial license, and the Question Bank may not be posted online/cached
  without written permission). Frame AceBoard as **noncommercial / educational**,
  be specific, and don't overstate scale. Worst case they say no and nothing
  changes; best case you get a real, defensible yes.

---

>   modification of the questions.

> alternative you do permit — for example, a defined quota of questions, a
> required attribution/notice format, or an approved API/integration path.
>

> third-party tools do. Please let me know what is possible and what terms would
> apply.

> **[Your name]**
> **[Email]** · **[AceBoard URL]**
